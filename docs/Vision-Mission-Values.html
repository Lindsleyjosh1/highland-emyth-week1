Core Values

How We Operate

1. Patient First

"Every decision starts with: What's best for the patient?"

Examples: Recommending against treatment when not appropriate | Making processes easy for patients | Following up proactively | Speaking honestly about expectations

2. Excellence in Everything

"We don't do average. Every touchpoint reflects Highland quality."

Examples: Responding within 4 hours | Charts that would impress any peer reviewer | Clear, professional communications | Systems that work reliably every time

3. Radical Ownership

"We own our outcomes. No excuses, no blame, just solutions."

Examples: "I'll fix it" instead of "That's not my job" | Taking responsibility for mistakes | Suggesting improvements instead of complaining

4. Continuous Improvement

"Today's best is tomorrow's baseline. We never stop getting better."

Examples: Documenting processes so they can be improved | Seeking feedback actively | Learning from every mistake

5. Transparent Communication

"We say what we mean, share what matters, and ask when uncertain."

Examples: Honest conversations about performance | Sharing business information with the team | Clear expectations and feedback

Values Decision Test

When facing a decision, ask:

  1. Patient First: Does this serve the patient's best interest?
  2. Excellence: Is this up to Highland standards?
  3. Ownership: Am I taking responsibility for the outcome?
  4. Improvement: Does this make us better long-term?
  5. Transparency: Am I being honest and clear?

If you can answer "yes" to all five, proceed. If not, reconsider.
