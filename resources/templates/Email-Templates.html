1. Pre-Week Announcement

Send this email to the team on Friday before Week 1 begins.

Pre-Week Announcement Email
To: Highland Team Subject: Exciting Week Ahead - E-Myth Week 1 Kickoff! Send: Friday before Week 1

Hi Team,

I wanted to give you a heads up about next week. We're officially kicking off something I'm really excited about - our E-Myth implementation.

What is E-Myth?
It's a framework for building a business that runs on systems rather than chaos. For Highland, this means creating clear roles, documented processes, and a foundation that lets us grow while making everyone's job easier.

What to expect next week:

  • Tuesday: Team kickoff meeting (30-45 min) - I'll share the vision and what this means for everyone
  • Wednesday: 1:1 conversations to discuss your role and get your input
  • Thursday: Org chart workshop as a team
  • Friday: Quick debrief to wrap up Week 1

Why should you care?
This isn't about adding more work - it's about clarity. You'll know exactly what's expected, have input into how your role is defined, and help build systems that make Highland a better place to work.

I'll share more details on Tuesday. Looking forward to building this together!

- [Your name]

2. Post-Kickoff Summary

Send this email Tuesday evening after the kickoff meeting.

Post-Kickoff Summary Email
To: Highland Team Subject: Great Kickoff Today - Next Steps Send: Tuesday evening (Day 2)

Hi Team,

Thank you for your engagement in today's kickoff! I'm excited about the energy and questions you brought.

Key takeaways from today:

  • E-Myth is about building systems that make our work clearer and more consistent
  • We're charting positions, not people - one person can wear multiple hats
  • Your input matters - you know your role better than anyone
  • As Highland grows, everyone's role grows with it

This week's schedule:

  • Tomorrow (Wednesday): 1:1 conversations - I'll reach out to schedule your time
  • Thursday: Org chart workshop at [TIME]
  • Friday: Quick debrief at [TIME]

Your assignment:
Think about your role before our 1:1. What do you actually do day-to-day? What decisions do you make? What metrics would fairly measure your success? Your honest input will shape your position contract.

Questions? Grab me anytime this week.

- [Your name]

Send this after each 1:1 conversation to confirm what was discussed.

1:1 Follow-up Email
To: [Team member name] Subject: Follow-up: Our 1:1 Conversation Send: Same day as 1:1

Hi [Name],

Thanks for the great conversation today! I appreciate your candor and insights about your role.

What we discussed:

  • Your primary position: [Position title]
  • Key accountabilities we identified: [List 2-3 main points]
  • Metrics we're considering: [List metrics discussed]
  • Concerns/questions you raised: [Any concerns]

Next steps:

  • I'll draft your position contract based on our discussion
  • We'll review it together before you sign
  • Your assignment: Complete your position contract draft by [Date - usually Feb 12]

Reminder: Thursday's org chart workshop at [TIME]

If you think of anything else about your role, let me know!

- [Your name]
