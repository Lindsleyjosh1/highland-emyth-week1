E-Myth Org Chart Principles

The Three Levels

Level Focus Key Question
Visionary Where are we going? "What if...?"
Management How do we get there? "How do we make this work?"
Execution Getting it done daily "What needs to happen today?"

Rules for Org Charts

  • Chart positions, not people - A position exists even if unfilled
  • One person, multiple hats - Common in growing businesses
  • Clear reporting lines - Everyone knows who to escalate to
  • Positions enable growth - As Highland grows, positions can be filled by new team members
